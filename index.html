👻 World's Scariest Places 👻

Enter at your own risk...

🕯️ The Catacombs of Paris, France

Dark tunnels stacked with the bones of six million souls. Whispers echo in the silence...

🏚️ The Stanley Hotel, USA

The hotel that inspired *The Shining*. Ghostly piano music and spectral figures haunt its halls.

🏰 Bran Castle, Romania

Known as Dracula’s Castle. Visitors feel watched and claim to see apparitions lurking within the stone walls.

🌲 Aokigahara Forest, Japan

Dense, quiet, and eerie. Compasses spin wildly, and the forest is said to trap souls forever.

🏝️ Poveglia Island, Italy

Plague victims and asylum patients haunt this cursed island. Fishermen refuse to approach it.

⚰️ Sleep tight... if you can. ⚰️
